**Intro:**
> Here's what happens when you turn Plaibook on.

### Beat 01: Every interaction is analyzed and graded.

> Every sales call is scored against your quality control checkpoints the moment it ends. Not a sample. Not the ones your reps choose to report. Every call, every rep, every day.

**Demo:** Live-scrolling call feed with expandable detail panels showing transcripts, QC checkpoint scores, and AI coaching notes

**Caption:** Your entire team scored in real time. No spreadsheets. No sampling.

### Beat 02: Know how customers are won and lost.

> See which objections are killing deals, what your top reps do differently, and where coaching will move the needle. Click any objection to drill into the calls behind it.

**Demo:** Interactive 3-level drilldown — objection revenue chart → rep/call breakdown → call detail with transcript and coaching

**Caption:** Revenue lost by objection. What worked. What didn't. All the way down to the call.

### Beat 03: Close missed deals.

> When a lead doesn't close on the call, Plaibook picks it up over text — handles objections, answers questions, and closes the deal. Contract signed, payment collected, automatically.

**Demo:** Phone-frame SMS conversation showing an AI recovering a lost lead (Jennifer Walsh), overcoming a price objection, booking an appointment, and collecting payment
